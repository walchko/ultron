## MK3S

- Form factor: Rectangular
- Origin: Lower Left
- Heated bed: yes
- Width (X): 250 mm
- Depth (Y): 210 mm
- Height (Z): 200 mm
- Custom bounding box: X: 0/250, Y: -4/210, Z: 0/200
- Maximum X speed: 6000 mm/min
- Maximum Y speed: 6000 mm/min
- Maximum Z speed: 200 mm/min
- E (extruder) feed rate: 300 mm/min
- Nozzle: 0.4 mm
- Number of Extruders: 1
